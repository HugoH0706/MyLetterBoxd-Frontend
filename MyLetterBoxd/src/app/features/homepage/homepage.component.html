About MyLetterBoxd

MyLetterBoxd is a comprehensive entertainment tracker designed for enthusiasts who love to keep a record of their media consumption. Whether it's movies, series, or books, our platform offers a seamless way to track and manage your lists. With future updates, we aim to include music, podcasts, and other forms of entertainment.
